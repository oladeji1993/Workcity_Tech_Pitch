APPLICATION & SELECTION

Applicants will submit 90-second videos pitching their startup idea. All applications are recorded and passed to an independent panel of judges for assessment.

ASSESSMENT

Our esteemed panel of guest judges will score each startup based on their application video. The video should emphasize the problem or need your company solves, your product/service offering, and its benefits to consumers.

This is a 3minuites presentation. Just you and your ideas, no pitch decks so focus on your verbal delivery and make the most of it.
